Atglen, PA

Location

Atglen is a rather small town in Chester County, Pennsylvania. Atglen is located an hour outside of Philadelphia, approximately 155.9 miles away.

About

Weather

In Atglen, you experience all four seasons with occasional snow in the winters and hot summers. Atglen also sees cool temperatures in both the spring and fall.

Population

There is a population of 1,306 according to records in 2021.

Sports

There aren't many popular sports in Atglen, PA. But, local football teams attract many fans. There are also many Philadelphia fans. Eagles fans are all over the town.

Things To Do
